Discover Story

What is Eris Cafe?

A restaurant, coffee house, and bar. A place for musicians, activists, and poets to meet. We offer culture and fine dining, but we always make sure there are affordable but filling plates available.

Your Creative Home

If you are an artist looking to meet others and hone your craft while enjoying delicious food, coffee, and cocktails? Eris cafe is looking for you, too!
